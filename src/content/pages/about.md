This site is a home for projects that benefit from more context than a social
post or portfolio thumbnail can provide. It combines professional work,
experiments, photography, video, and editorial writing in a single static,
maintainable publication.

The goal is straightforward: build useful things, document them honestly, and
make the finished work easy to explore.
